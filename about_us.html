Who We Are

Welcome to All Care Dental!

Since our founding, All Care Dental has been committed to enhancing smiles and improving oral health with the latest in dental technology and patient care. We continuously invest in cutting-edge advancements, high safety protocols, and staff training to provide you with easy access to world-class dental treatments—all under one roof.

At All Care Dental, we prioritize a relaxed, timely, and personalized experience. To ensure this, we limit the number of appointments each day, so you can enjoy quality care in our modern, state-of-the-art clinic. Our facilities include:

  • Advanced Dentistry for comprehensive, high-quality treatments.
  • Top-notch safety standards to ensure your well-being at every visit.
  • Caring and skilled staff who are dedicated to delivering exceptional results.
  • Specialized services in dental implants, orthodontics, and general dentistry.
  • Convenient appointment scheduling designed to fit your lifestyle.
  • Strict hygiene practices with sterile instruments and per-patient use.
  • Air purification systems to maintain a safe and clean environment for all.
  • ISO 9001:2015 certified clinic ensuring the highest standards of quality care..
  • Certified X-ray rooms by the Atomic Energy Regulatory Council of Sri Lanka.

We are here to make your dental care experience as comfortable and effective as possible!
