General Dentistry

We truly take care of your teeth. Here at Align Dental Care, we provide general dentistry services in Sri Lanka such as dental cleaning, routine checkups, teeth whitening, and dental fillings.

From routine checkups to tooth extractions and dental fillings, our dentist will cultivate a personalized oral treatment plan by determining your overall health to provide you the best possible results.

Why Align Dental Care?

We offer a range of oral services that mainly focus on the restorative and preventive care of your gums and teeth. We have the latest modern dental technology, handled by our experienced dentist and oral hygienist, ensuring the best dental care treatments in Sri Lanka every time.

Preventive Services

Preventive services help you maintain good dental health by blocking disease before it affects your mouth. Routine examinations (including x-rays) and efficient teeth cleanings form the foundation of prevention. We recommend regular dental checkups to prevent plaque buildup, cavities, tooth decay, and various gum problems.

Restorative Services

When an issue is identified, our dentist will ensure that you receive appropriate treatment promptly. Most dental restorations involve removing decayed areas of a tooth and filling the damaged tooth.

Overall Oral Health Concerns

Your oral health is often a reflection of your overall health. Certain oral problems may indicate issues in other parts of your body. We recommend regular checkups at least twice a year to monitor your oral health.

Teeth Cleaning

Good oral hygiene is essential not only for aesthetics but also to prevent cavities, tartar buildup, and gum disease. Poor oral hygiene can lead to various dental and medical problems.

Dental Filling

Cavities develop into tiny holes and can cause permanent damage to teeth. Left untreated, they can lead to various dental issues, including tooth loss. Sensitivity to sweet, hot, or cold foods and beverages is a sign that it's time to visit your dentist.

Tooth Extraction

Tooth extraction is only considered when a tooth is badly damaged and cannot be repaired. It also helps prevent the spread of infection from a decayed tooth to other parts of your mouth. Tooth extraction is also necessary for removing impacted wisdom teeth.

Treatment for Bad Breath

The primary cause of bad breath is poor dental hygiene. Regular dental checkups and teeth cleaning can help you overcome it. Persistent bad breath can result from tooth decay, gum disease, oral infections, or other systemic health issues.
