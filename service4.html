What is a Smile Makeover?

Improving your smile to make it more meaningful and beautiful by hiding the factors that make your smile unpleasant. It is the most important day of your life. The photos that are going to be taken on this special day are a precious memory that you will preserve with pride forever. Our treatment for smile makeovers in Sri Lanka gives you a healthy smile and pearly white teeth that will make your day more beautiful.

We have the experience and technology to design bridal smile makeovers that give a dramatic change to the display of your smile.

Popular Bridal Smile Makeover Choices for You

Teeth Whitening Option:

This is a quick and easy option that you can do before your wedding to smile at your best. You may feel fearful about your smile because of discoloration or stains on the teeth. Our professional teeth whitening treatment will be completed within an hour, giving you pearly white teeth.

Invisalign if You Plan Ahead:

If you are planning your wedding well in advance, Invisalign is a great option. This treatment generally takes nine months to one year. Choosing Invisalign is the ultimate option as it is removable, so you can remove it while taking engagement photos or whenever necessary. Your teeth will be ideal for the wedding day and after too.

Porcelain Veneers for a Hollywood Smile:

Smile like a Hollywood star at your wedding. Porcelain veneers are a great option for a beautiful smile makeover. Aesthetic complaints such as chips, gaps, cracks, stains, or misalignment will be completely corrected, and your smile will be reshaped. A flattering size, shade, and shape of your teeth will make you look beautiful on your wedding day.
