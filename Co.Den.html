Cosmetic Dentistry

Broken tooth, gap in teeth, or discoloration of your teeth affects your confidence in smile, general appearance, and beauty. However, you need not worry anymore as we provide a wide range of cosmetic remedies that will fix all your problems providing you the best transformation that dentistry has to offer.

When do you need cosmetic dentistry?

  • If your tooth is chipped, cracked or decayed.
  • Missing tooth
  • Discolored tooth
  • Spacing in tooth
  • Cross bite, overbite and underbite
  • Gummy smile.

These are the treatments available to perfect your smile

Bridges

Bridges are used to close gaps between teeth. Dental bridges are supported by implants or natural tooth by adjusting two or more crowns that are kept on both sides of the gap.

Ceramic bridges

Ceramic bridges are made with strong, long-lasting material with an aesthetic feature. They are far superior to normally used porcelain-fused metal bridges.

Zirconia bridges

Zirconia crowns are popularly used nowadays to cover chipped and stained tooth. These zirconia materials do not have allergic reactions too. They are tough, durable, and easy to wear and blend with your existing set of teeth.

Porcelain veneers

Porcelain veneers are a great option for closing gaps between the teeth. The number of porcelain requires depends on the amount of gap in teeth. If you have a larger gap, more amount of porcelain is used to give a uniform look to teeth.
