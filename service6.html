First Visit

On your first visit, our orthodontist will review your condition and discuss the outcome you expect. After reviewing your records, the orthodontist will provide a personalized treatment plan and custom-made appliance designed specifically for you.

Though ready-made appliances are available, you will receive a custom appliance that is more effective and shortens the treatment time. We use a dedicated orthodontic system where we input all patient data to check viability and design the appliance and treatment plan. Advice will be given on eating habits and cleaning. Regular adjustments will be made to observe progress.

Orthodontic Treatments Offered by Align Orthodontics

The orthodontists at Align Orthodontics use high-quality metal braces to provide controlled orthodontic movements for a successful treatment.

These tooth-colored braces offer better aesthetics compared to metal braces. They are less visible and more subtle.

Fully customized, computer-aided design treatment with removable and invisible aligners. Maintenance is easier compared to the other two options.

At What Age Should You Get Braces?

Many people believe that braces are only for kids, but that's not true. While the ideal time for braces is during childhood, adults with healthy teeth and gums can also undergo braces treatment. Orthodontic treatment is more effective in children due to their flexible bones and growth phase. Parents are encouraged to bring their children to the orthodontist when their first permanent teeth erupt.
