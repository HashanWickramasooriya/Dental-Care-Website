Suffering from missing teeth? – Bring back confidence!

A dental implant is the best choice for your problem. It is like a root of the tooth made up of titanium that looks like a screw and that will be fused into the jawbone beneath the gum line to support one or more false teeth.

Dental Implants with the Latest Digital Scanning Technology

We have updated digital scanning technology, and we are proud to have one of the world’s best scanners. We offer top-class treatment for dental implants in Sri Lanka. There is no better way to capture the location and perfect angle of your implant than with scanning bodies. Instead of taking plaster impressions for placing implants, our digital scanner scans your mouth completely, delivering high precision and predictable outcomes.

The machine works by creating a patient-specific, extremely accurate digital image of a dental surface that is then stored as a computer file. The scan is translated into a 3D digital representation using specialized software.

First, a method called slicing is done, where the original 3D image is split into thin horizontal layers. These layers are then passed to 3D printers, where the physical model will be printed layer by layer before completion of the actual 3D model.

The complete digital image is sent to a milling machine, which starts with a solid piece of material. The milling machine carves the new 3D physical model out of that block of material.

Why it is Needed?

Tooth implants are a form of beauty in dentistry. They can act and perform like real teeth. It is common to lose the strength of the tooth with aging, which may lead to tooth loss. In cases where a supporting tooth is missing, the cheek can retract. With dental implants, your facial shape is preserved, and it is an excellent option for those who care about oral health but have lost teeth due to injury.
